About Plenary Sessions

Plenary speakers at the 2026 AISC Conference will present innovative research spanning multiple areas of statistics and combinatorics. These sessions are designed to bring the entire conference community together to explore emerging methodologies, novel applications, and important challenges in the field.

Each plenary session offers an opportunity to engage with leading researchers whose work bridges theoretical advances with practical applications, fostering interdisciplinary collaboration and sparking new research directions.

Session Topics

Plenary sessions will cover diverse topics including but not limited to:

  • Advances in statistical methodology and theory
  • Emerging applications in data science and machine learning
  • Interdisciplinary research combining statistics with other fields
  • Computational methods for complex data analysis
  • Statistical approaches to contemporary challenges
