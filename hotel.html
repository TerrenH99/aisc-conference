Official Conference Hotel

We are pleased to partner with the Hilton Garden Inn Lafayette/Cajundome as the official hotel for the 2026 AISC Conference. Located just minutes from the Louisiana Immersive Technologies Enterprise (LITE), this hotel offers convenient access to the conference venue and comfortable accommodations for all attendees.
