Conference Venue

The 2026 AISC Conference will be held at the Louisiana Immersive Technologies Enterprise (LITE), a state-of-the-art facility featuring a 175-seat theatre, breakout rooms, and cutting-edge technology. LITE is conveniently located near the Cajundome with easy access to downtown Lafayette and I-10.

Louisiana Immersive Technologies Enterprise (LITE)

Address: 537 Cajundome Blvd, Suite 101, Lafayette, LA 70506

Phone: (337) 456-1824

Website: lite.louisiana.edu

Conference Hotel

We are pleased to announce that the official conference hotel is the Hilton Garden Inn Lafayette/Cajundome. This conveniently located hotel offers comfortable accommodations and is just minutes from the conference venue.

Explore Lafayette

While attending the conference, take time to experience Lafayette's rich culture, world-famous cuisine, and vibrant music scene. Known as the heart of Cajun and Creole country, Lafayette offers unique attractions including:

Cuisine: Enjoy authentic Cajun and Creole dishes at local restaurants

Music: Experience live Cajun, Zydeco, and Louisiana music

Culture: Visit Vermilionville, Acadian Village, and the Acadiana Center for the Arts

Nature: Explore the unique South Louisiana wetlands and swamp tours
